Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Overview

AudienceW provides customer messaging tools for businesses. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when businesses use our platform.

Information we collect

We may collect business account information, user profile information, customer conversation metadata, message content, labels, notes, template information, usage logs, and support communications.

How we use information

We use information to provide the service, process messages, operate shared inbox features, support users, improve platform reliability, protect against abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Data sharing

We may share data with service providers that help us host, secure, monitor, and operate the platform. When using WhatsApp Business features, data may be processed through Meta and WhatsApp Business Platform services according to their applicable terms.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact us at privacy@audiencew.com.
